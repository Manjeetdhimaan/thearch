About

Manmeet Arora

Contriving Modern Marvels. Manmeet Arora , a vibrant woman , with strong roots and sky high vision , is successfully operating as the Principal Architect at The Arch. She is the founding principal and design director of a design-and-build firm that works across architecture, interiors and landscaping. Putting her thoughts and work in coherence , she has handled a variety of projects including residences, commercial developments, retail outlets, restaurants, spas and gyms.
