Manmeet Arora


Contriving Modern Marvels. Manmeet Arora, a vibrant woman, with strong roots and sky high vision, is successfully operating as the Principal Architect at The Arch. She is the founding principal and design director of a design-and-build firm that works across architecture, interiors and landscaping.
